USAGE:
  gitmonkey welcome [--skip-intro] [--reset]

OPTIONS:
  --skip-intro    Bypass animations and welcome sequence
  --reset         Force welcome screen to display regardless of previous runs

The welcome command initializes or reinitializes the onboarding experience.
It creates configuration in ~/.gitmonkey/ for identity, theme and tone preferences.

Automatically executes on first run. Can be manually triggered to reset preferences
or redisplay the guided tour. Returns immediately if a subcommand is specified.
